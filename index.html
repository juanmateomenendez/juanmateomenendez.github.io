About

I started in motion graphics but have always had a passion for tech. As web projects kept finding their way into my work, I eventually leaned all the way in and became a full-stack developer. Most recently, I built a platform that helps indie filmmakers sell merch through cross-pollination of their different films, and before that I created the website for the FilmADE Fund, supporting the distribution of 30 grants ranging from $5,000 to $50,000. Marrying motion graphics with web design, I enjoy building thoughtful and simple websites that support creative communities.
